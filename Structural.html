How To Change Disck Brake Pads on a Car

Changing your own brakes cane be easy, fun and save you big money.

Tools and Parts Needed

  • Replacement Brake Pads
  • Jack and jack stands
  • Lung wrench
  • C Clamp
  • Scocket rachet or wrench
  • Caliper grease
  • Anti Brake Squeal
  • Optional plastic gloves
